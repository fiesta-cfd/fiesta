********************************************************************************
What is Fiesta?
********************************************************************************
Fiesta is a computational fluid dynamics solver specialized for multi-scale 
compressible flows.

********************************************************************************
How Does Fiesta Work?
********************************************************************************
Fiesta solves the Navier-Stokes and Euler equations on GPUs and CPUs using
the Kokkos C++ Performance Portability Ecosystem.

********************************************************************************
Why Was Fiesta Written?
********************************************************************************
Fiesta was written to address a lack of modern, gpu-capable open source 
compressible flow solvers.

********************************************************************************
Who is Fiesta?
********************************************************************************
Fiesta was developed at the University of New Mexico by Brian Romero.
